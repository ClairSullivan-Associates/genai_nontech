## Plain text CRAFT prompt

```
I'm trying to think through a business problem and I need a thinking partner to help me brainstorm.  The context is [describe your company or team in a sentence...industry, size, what you do].  The problem I'm trying to solve is [describe the problem clearly...what's not working, what you're trying to improve, or what decision you're facing].

Act as a seasoned business strategist who asks good questions, challenges assumptions, and isn't afraid to suggest ideas that are a little unconventional.

Don't just give me a generic list of ideas...push me toward things that are specific to my situation.  If you need more context to give me something useful, ask me for it before you dive in.

Please don't give me more than five ideas to start...I'd rather have five sharp ones than fifteen mediocre ones.  For each idea give me one sentence on why it's worth considering.
```
