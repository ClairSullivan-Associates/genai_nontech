## Plain text CRAFT prompt

```
I am a [your role] at a [describe your organization in a sentence] and I need an image for [describe where and how it will be used, e.g. a presentation slide, a report cover, an onboarding deck, an internal newsletter]. The image should [describe the feeling or purpose it needs to serve, e.g. put a human face on the data, welcome new employees, illustrate a key theme].

Act as a [type of photographer or illustrator, e.g. documentary photographer, corporate editorial photographer, editorial illustrator] shooting for a [describe the publication or brand standard you want to evoke, e.g. respected regional publication, Fortune 500 annual report, progressive people-first brand] rather than a [describe what you want to avoid sounding like, e.g. generic stock photo library, charity campaign, corporate brochure].

Avoid [list what you do not want: posed or forced expressions, text or watermarks, cluttered backgrounds, clichéd compositions like handshakes or people pointing at whiteboards, distorted faces or hands, anything that feels staged or disconnected from real life].

[Describe the scene in concrete visual terms: who is in the image and what are they doing, where are they, what expressions or body language are visible, what objects or environmental details are present, what the light source is and what quality of light it creates, and what emotion or energy the scene should convey.]

[Shot type, e.g. wide shot / medium shot / close up], 16:9 aspect ratio, [describe the color palette and overall visual tone], [describe depth of field or focus preference].

If the first result feels too [posed / cold / staged / cluttered], follow up in the same thread asking to [specific tweak, e.g. shift the angle, change the lighting, make the moment more candid, adjust the composition] while keeping everything else the same.
```
