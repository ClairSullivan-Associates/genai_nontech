## Plain text CRAFT prompt

```
I need to learn the most important things about [enter the subject] as fast as possible. [Provide context on why you need to learn this and how you will use it].  [Provide a brief description of what you already know that might be relevant].

Act as an experienced [subject area] coach who specializes in helping first time [job title] get up to speed quickly...you know what actually matters in the first 30 days and what can wait.

Teach me 80% of what I need to know in 20% of the time. Don't give me a textbook...give me the highest leverage things a first time [job title] gets wrong and what to do instead. Keep it to the five most important things and be specific...I need to be functional by tomorrow morning not polished by next year.
```
