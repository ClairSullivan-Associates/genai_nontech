## Plain text CRAFT prompt

```
I need to create a standard operating procedure for [describe the process in one or two sentences...what it is, who does it, and when it happens]. This process takes place at [describe your company or department in a sentence...industry, size, type of work you do].

Act as an experienced operations manager who is skilled at taking loosely described processes and turning them into clear, professional SOPs that a new employee could follow on day one.

Here are the steps I know are part of this process... [list the steps you know, even if they are rough or out of order...don't worry about making them perfect]

Using those steps as the foundation, fill in any gaps where a logical step is clearly missing, suggest any best practices a professional in this field would typically include, and flag anything that seems unclear or that you made an assumption about so I can verify it.

Format it as a proper SOP with a title, a brief purpose statement, a list of who this applies to, numbered steps in order, and any notes or warnings where relevant.

Keep the language clear and direct...this should be something a real employee would actually read and use, not a corporate document nobody opens.

[paste or list your process steps here]
```
