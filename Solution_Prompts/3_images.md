## Plain text CRAFT prompt

### Prompt 1

```
I work in People and Culture at a large healthcare organization and need a warm, human, and inclusive image for the welcome section of a new hire onboarding deck. 

Act as an editorial photographer shooting for a progressive, people-first healthcare brand, with the warmth and authenticity of a modern employee experience campaign rather than a clinical or corporate stock photo library. Avoid sterile hospital settings, posed smiles, cluttered desks, text, watermarks, and anything that feels cold or institutional. 

A young woman of color in her late 20s sitting down at a clean bright desk for the first time, arranging a few personal items next to her laptop, a soft genuine smile on her face suggesting quiet excitement, warm natural light coming from a window to her left, a modern open plan office with blurred colleagues working in the background suggesting a welcoming busy environment, a small welcome plant and a branded notebook visible on the corner of the desk. Wide shot, 16:9 aspect ratio, warm natural color palette of soft whites, greens, and warm wood tones, shallow depth of field. If the first result feels too posed, follow up in the same thread asking to capture her looking down at her desk rather than at the camera to create a more candid authentic moment.
```

### Prompt 2

```
Now generate a second image in exactly the same visual style, color palette, lighting, and energy as the first image. This time show a diverse group of four colleagues in a bright modern break room gathered around a new team member, mid-conversation and mid-laugh, one person leaning in to shake hands warmly while others smile naturally, a mix of ages and ethnicities, casual professional attire, coffee cups and a small plant on the counter in the background. Medium shot, 16:9 aspect ratio.
```

### Prompt 3

```
Now generate a third image maintaining the exact same visual style, color palette, lighting, and energy as the first two images. This time show a diverse group of six employees seated around a large bright table in a modern training room, one confident facilitator standing at the front gesturing toward a clean presentation screen, participants leaning forward with open notebooks and laptops, expressions curious and engaged. Wide shot, 16:9 aspect ratio.
```
