## Plain text CRAFT prompt

```
I'm trying to think through a management challenge and I need a thinking partner to help me brainstorm. I manage a team that is fully remote and spread across multiple time zones. We get the work done but something is missing...connection, trust, momentum...I can't quite put my finger on it, but the team doesn't feel as cohesive as I'd like and I'm not sure what I as the manager can do differently.

Act as a seasoned business strategist and leadership coach who asks good questions, challenges assumptions, and isn't afraid to suggest ideas that are a little unconventional.

Don't give me a generic list of remote work tips I could find in any article. Push me toward things that are specific to my situation as a manager. If you need more context about my team or how we currently work together before you can give me something useful, ask me for it before you dive in.

Please don't give me more than five ideas to start. I'd rather have five sharp ones than fifteen mediocre ones. For each idea give me one sentence on why it's worth considering.
```
