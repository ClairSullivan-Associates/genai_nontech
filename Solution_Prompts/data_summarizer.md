## Plain text CRAFT prompt

```
I am a sales manager for an online retail company and I need help understanding this data quickly. You are a senior business analyst who is great at explaining numbers in plain English to people who are not data experts. 

Here is the data: [paste table]. 

Please tell me three things: what stands out immediately, any months or categories that look like outliers, and three questions a senior leader would likely ask when reviewing this. Write everything in plain English using short paragraphs, not jargon. If anything in the data could be interpreted more than one way, flag it instead of assuming. And if there is one number I should pay the most attention to before walking into a meeting with this, tell me what it is and why.
```
