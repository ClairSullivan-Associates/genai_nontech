## Plain text CRAFT prompt

```
I am a financial analyst at a regional bank preparing an annual community impact report for shareholders, board members, and local media, and I need an image that puts a human face on our community grants data without feeling manipulative or staged. 

Act as a documentary photographer shooting for a respected regional publication, with the authenticity and warmth of a National Geographic community story rather than a charity campaign or corporate social responsibility brochure. Avoid anything that looks posed, pitying, or performative, and avoid text, watermarks, logos, and overly polished settings that feel disconnected from real community life. 

A multigenerational group of six to eight community members of diverse ages and ethnicities working together outdoors in a bright well-maintained community garden, some kneeling in the soil planting seedlings with dirt on their hands, others passing tools or water to each other, an older man laughing with a teenage girl beside a raised garden bed, genuine expressions of effort and connection rather than awareness of the camera, a warm sunny afternoon casting golden natural light across the scene, modest homes and trees softly blurred in the background grounding the image in a real neighborhood rather than an abstract space. Wide shot, 16:9 aspect ratio, warm earthy color palette of greens, browns, and golden yellows, natural depth of field keeping the full group in focus. 
```
