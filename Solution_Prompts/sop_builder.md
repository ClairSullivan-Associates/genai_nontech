## Plain text CRAFT prompt

```
I need to create a standard operating procedure for hiring a new staff member onto my team. This process covers everything from recognizing a vacancy through the first day of onboarding, and it is owned by me as the hiring manager. This process takes place at a mid-sized professional services firm where I manage a team of eight and work closely with HR and our department director to get new hires approved and brought on board.

Act as an experienced operations manager who is skilled at taking loosely described processes and turning them into clear, professional SOPs that a new employee could follow on day one.

Here are the steps I know are part of this process: I recognize I have a vacancy or a new headcount need, I get verbal approval from my director, I send a job description to HR, HR posts the role, resumes come to me, I review them and pick candidates to interview, I schedule and run first-round interviews, I loop in two team members for a second round, I pick a finalist, HR handles the offer, and then I set up their first week.

Using those steps as the foundation, fill in any gaps where a logical step is clearly missing, suggest any best practices a hiring manager in a professional services environment would typically include, and flag anything that seems unclear or that you made an assumption about so I can verify it.

Format it as a proper SOP with a title, a brief purpose statement, a list of who this applies to, numbered steps in order, and any notes or warnings where relevant. Keep the language clear and direct. This should be something a real manager would actually hand to a new team lead, not a corporate document nobody opens.
```
